# Voices Protocol
*Card-building templates (A–F). At runtime the **character card** wins. Do not load this file every draft — only when creating/editing cards.*

Runtime bans and polarization: [Rules_Index.md](../Rules_Index.md) §4.  
Matrix defaults: [Main.md](../Main.md) §5.

---

## I. Rules of the Room (short)

- Body before insight; never name trauma/realm/bias on the page
- No operator-cool clinical drift under pressure
- Lean natural speech under stakes; no document-register bleed in dialogue
- Polarize volume/rhythm between characters

## III. Audit (before saving a card or draft)

1. Two characters interchangeable? Rebuild.  
2. Banned fillers overused? (`looked at`, `for a moment`, `said quietly`, `genuinely`)  
3. Abstract emotion → physical beat  
4. Dialogue free of paperwork jargon unless reading aloud
