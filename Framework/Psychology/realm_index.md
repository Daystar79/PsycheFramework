## The Great Wheel (Ten Realms Index)

Somatic and psychological profile for all 10 Realms. Load this index by default with Main + Rules_Index + on-scene cards.

| Realm | Somatic Focus | Release (Passage) Somatics | Bracing (Remnant) Somatics | Key / Trigger |
|:---:|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **I**<br>Origin | Center / Shoulders | Unrehearsed shoulder drop; dropping performance self | Narrating own calm; managing appearance of stability | "I am here before the room." |
| **II**<br>Form | Hands / Craft | Unhurried craft; allowing mistakes to stay; raw touch | Rigid, perfect repetition; mechanical perfection | "The craft belongs to the hands." |
| **III**<br>Identity | Chest / Sternum | Own current before the room; independent emotional weight | Managing everyone else's tension; emotional compliance | "I hold my own weight." |
| **IV**<br>Will | Posture / Spine | Steady, non-urgent want; relaxed posture | Urgency as purpose; rigid holding to command/framework | "Wanting without framework." |
| **V**<br>Echoes | Ears / Hearing | Receiving raw feedback; listening without defense | Confirmation-only hearing; parsing inputs for threat | "I hear what is, not what I fear." |
| **VI**<br>Compassion | Chest / Inhale | Two truths, same space; holding boundaries while caring | Disappearing into other's need; savior complex merge | "I am here. So are you." |
| **VII**<br>Presence | Feet / Ground | Full body land; feeling weight honestly | Performed presence; controlled, timed breathing | "The moment is already here." |
| **VIII**<br>Integration | Partitions / Rhythm | Dropping role-partitions; consistent register across rooms | Hyper-efficient code-switching; context compartmentalization | "There is nothing left to add." |
| **IX**<br>Threshold | Fingers / Breath | Stepping while trembling; moving despite fear | Waiting for fear to leave; physical freezing | "Step while the trembling lasts." |
| **X**<br>Return | Hands / Posture | Hands open as in sleep; unmanaged opening | Performed open hands; closed grip masked as open | "Hands open, nothing to keep." |
